How to Make an Awesome Pizza

Assembly Time: 20 minutes
Awesomeness Rating: 5% awesome

Are you looking to make an incredible and delicious pizza and don't know where to start? You've come to the right place. Follow these simple steps to assemble the PERFECT pizza pie!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Dough
  • Sauce of Choice
  • Cheese of Choice (the only right answer is low-moisture mozzarella)
  • Toppings of Choice

  1. Form dough with hands.

    Stretch out dough into a circular shape. Do not use a rolling pin. Follow video for more information.

  2. Spread sauce.

    Spread sauce evenly around the pie, leaving a naked circular edge for the crust. Be careful not to oversauce your pie. Using less sauce than what feels right will give you the perfect pie.

    Feel free to use any sauce of choice.

  3. Add cheese.

    Spread cheese evenly around entire pizza. If the sauce peeks through the cheese, that means you have the perfect amount.

  4. Add toppings.

    This is the phase where you can add any meats or vegatables of your choice. Cured salty meats and veggies that like some heat are always good. Some classics that work well are pepperoni, mushrooms, and olives.

  5. Final touches.

    Before you cook your pizza this is a good time to add or fix anything else. Make sure your toppings are spread evenly. Add more of one ingredient or take away anything. Drizzle some olive oil or add a sprinke of chili flakes.
